SI Units and Constants for Magnetism and Electromagnetism

Remember, if there’s not much new, review what you can already do!


Units

magnetic field (B) teslas [t] (tesla=Ns/Cm, newton second per coulomb meter)
magnetic permatiivity tesla meters per amp [Tm/A]
current density (J) amp per meter squared [A/m2]
magnetic flux ΦB weber [Wb] or tesla meter squared [Tm2]
inductance (L) henrys [H] (henry=TM2/A, tesla meter squared per amp)

Constants

magnetic permatiivity of free space (μ0) 4π*10-7
