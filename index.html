Welcome!

Please skim this info before proceeding:

This website contains resources for AP Physics C: Mechanics and Electricity and Magnetism (E & M). The explanations are written by me, a high schooler, but they are based on what I have learned from 2.5 years of physics classes, multiple textbooks which you can find the names of on the sources page, as well as various resources. The games and extra stuff have been helpful for me to visualize and play around with stuff, so see what works for you.

I'm working on updating this site with more content for other areas of physics (thermo, optics, etc) so check back in a few months if you're curious. I've also included some basic intros to concepts if you're in a pre-AP-C physics class.

Use what's helpful, ignore what isn't, and have fun :)
